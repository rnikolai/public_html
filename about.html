About Us

Who We Are

We Are the Best Accounting Agency

At Milinchuk CPA Professional Corporation, we’re more than just a financial services firm—we’re a team driven by a passion for innovation, integrity, and excellence. Our story is rooted in the visionary leadership of our founder, Anton Milinchuk, whose career journey embodies the relentless pursuit of professional and personal growth.

Anton’s foray into the world of accounting and finance began in 2016 when he joined BDO’s Markham office as a paid intern. It was there that he gained a robust foundation in public accounting—a challenging yet deeply rewarding experience that set the stage for his future endeavors.

Determined to push beyond traditional boundaries, Anton embraced a new challenge by partnering with his brother, Artem Milinchuk, as the first employee at the groundbreaking Ag-tech startup, FarmTogether. In this role, he was instrumental in establishing the company’s accounting and operational framework. His strategic insight and dedication were key to scaling the business from a modest $500K to an impressive $100MM AuM in under 24 months—a testament to his ability to transform challenges into opportunities.

In 2023, after earning his Canadian CPA designation, Anton set his sights on a new vision: to create his own practice. Thus, Milinchuk CPA Professional Corporation was born—a firm dedicated to providing personalized, forward-thinking financial solutions that empower both businesses and individuals to thrive in a dynamic economic landscape.

Today, our practice stands as a beacon of expertise and innovation. With Anton’s proven track record and unwavering commitment to excellence, we are uniquely positioned to offer strategic insights and tailored financial solutions designed to meet the evolving needs of our clients. We invite you to join us on this journey, as we continue to shape the future of financial success with transparency, dedication, and visionary leadership.
